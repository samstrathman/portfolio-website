About Me

I attended the University of Findlay, where I recieved an Associate degree in Computer Science. I have always taken a strong intrest in art and music, so after college I chose to work some odd jobs to give myself more room to continue developing my creative endeavors. While I enjoyed those jobs, they didn't give me that satisfying problem solving feeling that I experienced while coding.
            I began exploring other career options, looking for one that could strike the balance between the artistic and technical sides of my brain. When it finally hit me that front-end web development was the perfect compromise, I immediately began building websites. I have been working to ensure a solid understanding of the core elements (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, responsive web design principles, React.js, etc.), and I'm excited to delve further into these technologies as well as others!
