About Me

I attended the University of Findlay where I received an Associates degree in computer science. Right out of college I still didn't feel ready to enter the professional working world, so I spent a number of years working as a barista, then as the darkroom coordinator for a local screen printing shop. It was during this time that I decided I was finally ready to make the switch. After spending the last several months refreshing my memory and working through several online courses, and I've found my areas of interest currently to be in front-end web development. I've been doing a lot of work with the basics (HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, etc.) and I'm excited to delve further into these technologies as well as others!
